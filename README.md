#  Amazon Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

##  Project Overview
This project focuses on building **interactive Power BI dashboards** to analyze sales performance of Amazon products and Amazon Prime content.

The goal is to provide **data-driven insights** into:
- Product sales performance
- Customer engagement (reviews)
- Amazon Prime movie trends
- Business growth patterns

##  Dataset Information
This project uses **two datasets**:

1. **Amazon_Combined_Data.xlsx**
   -  Rows: **11,111**
   - Contains product sales, categories, reviews, and transactions

2. **Amazon Prime Movies.xlsx**
   -  Rows: **6,349**
   - Contains movie details, ratings, genres, and Prime-related data

---

## Dashboard 1: Amazon Product Sales Analysis

###  KPIs:
- YTD Sales  
- QTD Sales  
- YTD Products Sold  
- YTD Reviews  

###  Visualizations:
- Sales by Month (Trend Analysis)
- Sales by Week (Short-term trends)
- Sales by Product Category
- Top 5 Products by Sales
- Top 5 Products by Reviews

---

## Dashboard 2: Amazon Prime Analysis

### KPIs:
- Total Sales  
- Total Movies  
- Total Reviews  

### Visualizations:
- Movies by Release Year
- Sales by Movie Type
- Top 5 Movies by Sales
- Top 5 Movies by Reviews
- Sales by Quality (Prime Video, Blu-ray, etc.)

## Key Insights
- Seasonal spikes in product sales (Q4 growth)  
- Certain categories dominate revenue (e.g., shoes, cameras)  
- High correlation between reviews and sales  
- Prime content growth increased significantly after 2018  
- Specific movie types (e.g., CC, R-rated) generate higher sales
